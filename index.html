Emily and Greg bring a sophisticated blend of jazz, pop and soul to weddings, corporate events and private functions across Lincolnshire and the East Midlands.

Piano

An accomplished pianist with a deep passion for jazz harmony and a flair for making every performance feel uniquely tailored to the occasion. Greg's sensitive accompaniment and stylish arrangements provide the perfect musical backdrop for any event.

Frequently Asked

Questions We Hear Most

Can you learn a special song for our event?

Yes — we love learning bespoke songs for ceremonies, first dances and surprise moments. Just tell us your request in the booking form.

How long do you play for?

Our usual format is 2 x 45 minute sets, but we can tailor the timings to suit your event schedule.

Do you take song requests?

We're happy to take requests made in advance — just let us know when booking. We'll make sure your favourites are already in the setlist.

What do you need from the venue?

We need a small performance space, a power outlet, and a table for our equipment. We can handle the rest.
